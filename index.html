The User Experience

An email marketing campagin gets its start with the design process. As a designer I want the user to have a truly unique expereince when interacting with any email that I've worked on. This process starts with a responsive, mobile first approach and ends with a product that feels refreshing with a one of a kind touch that customers will want to interact with.

Quality Development

Once the design has been perfected the development process can begin. When it comes to developing and formatting an email you can be sure that your marketing campaign is in good hands. I have extensive expereince with writing code in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript - all formatted for responsiveness. To top off my skillset, I also have experience developing websites and application using advanced libraries and runtimes such as React, Node, and database architecture.

Rest and Relaxation

I love being creative with my work and exploring new technical avenues, but everyone needs a break eventually. When I'm not trying to perfect my craft as a developer you can find me shooting hoops at the local rec center, or maybe even taking a hike somewhere along the east coast on the beautiful Appalachain Trail.
